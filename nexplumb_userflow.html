Trust & Verification System

Trust is not a feature — it is the product. Every mechanism below is an engineering and design requirement. Without this system, the marketplace cannot function in Nigeria.

🪪
NIN Verification
NIMC API call at artisan onboarding. "ID Verified" shield badge on public profile. Artisan cannot receive jobs without NIN verified.
Phase 1 · Blocks Activation
🏦
BVN Linkage
Bank account linked via BVN at payout setup. Prevents fraud on withdrawals. Artisan cannot withdraw without BVN verified.
Phase 1 · Blocks Withdrawal
📱
Phone OTP
SMS OTP via Termii on all registrations. Auto-reads on Android. Blocks account creation if not verified.
Phase 1 · Blocks Registration
🛡
Escrow Payment
Paystack holds customer payment until job confirmed complete or 24h auto-release. Full refund on artisan no-show. No financial transaction without escrow.
Phase 1 · Mandatory
⭐
Two-Way Reviews
Customer rates artisan. Artisan rates customer. Auto-prompt 30 min post-completion. Below 3.0 over 15 jobs → account suspended.
Phase 1 · Quality Signal
👥
Guarantor System
2 guarantor names + phone numbers collected at artisan onboarding. Displayed as "Guarantor Verified" badge. Culturally familiar trust signal in Nigeria.
Phase 1 · Strongly Recommended
📸
Before/After Photos
Required for jobs above ₦15,000. Stored in escrow release flow. Auto-added to artisan portfolio with customer permission. Evidence in dispute resolution.
Phase 1 · Above ₦15K Threshold
⚖️
Dispute Resolution
Structured form + admin review + mandatory resolution note. Audit trail on all decisions. 48-hour SLA. Trust collapses if violated.
Phase 1 · SLA Critical
🎓
Nexplumb Trade Test
15-question skills test per trade. Pass = "Nexplumb Certified" badge. Retry in 7 days on fail. Certified artisans rank higher in search results.
Phase 2 · Quality Tier
🔒
Job Insurance Add-On
₦500 per booking. Covers property damage up to ₦500,000. Partner: AXA Mansard or Casava. Certificate auto-generated on purchase.
